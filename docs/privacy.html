Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how PokeBot ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our Discord bot (the "Service").

Information We Collect

PokeBot is designed to be privacy-friendly and collects minimal data. We collect the following information:

  • Discord User IDs: When you use our commands, we temporarily process your Discord User ID to facilitate command functionality (such as identifying who initiated a poke and who is being poked).
  • Command Usage: We may collect anonymous usage statistics about which commands are used and how frequently.
  • Server IDs: We process Discord Server IDs to ensure our bot functions correctly within your server.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for providing and improving the Service. Specifically:

  • To provide the core functionality of our commands
  • To monitor and analyze usage patterns to improve the bot
  • To respond to user support requests

Data Retention

PokeBot does not permanently store user content or messages. Command interactions are processed in memory and are not persisted after the interaction is complete.

Third-Party Services

Our Service uses the following third-party services:

  • Discord API: We use Discord's API to provide our Service. Discord has its own Privacy Policy.
  • Giphy API: We use Giphy's API to fetch GIFs for the poke command. Giphy has its own Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13, or under the age of 16 in the European Union. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
